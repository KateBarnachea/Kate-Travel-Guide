BAGUIO

Baguio City is regarded as Summer Capital of the Philippines because of its unusual cold weather all throughout the year. Baguio is one of the few places in the country which they can visit to escape the summer heat and enjoy a chilly weather. Baguio is pretty busy when it comes to Christmas season, especially the Filipino families which they love to visit and to enjoy the cool climate


LA UNION

La Union is a province in the Philippines located in the Ilocos Region in the island of Luzon

The first thing that comes to our mind upon the mention of La Union is the beach. If there is anything La Union famous for, it's the waves and pristines shores which it is a crowd favorite among surfer.


MANILA

Manila is the capital of the Philippines and the nation's center of Education, Business, and Transportation. Manila is the most populated area in the Philippines but there is a lot of people who visit this megacity because of its throngs of attraction. A huge city with several district articles containing sightseeing, restaurant, nightlife, and accomodation listings.


PANGASINAN

Pangasinan is a beautiful province that is often described as the gateway to Northern Luzon. From stunning islands to great beaches, Pangasinan has a lot of different destinations to keep you busy. You will be awestruck by the province's natural beauty.
